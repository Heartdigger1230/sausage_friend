Welcome to ianhoward.shop — The Internet’s #1 (and only) Ian Howard Fan Site.

This website started as a joke between friends. We bought a domain name we had no business owning, and somehow, it turned into this: a living monument to one man’s legacy — Ian (Shit) Howard.

Ian is many things: a Sonic fan, a bathroom enthusiast, unfairly good looking, and probably autistic!

Here, you’ll find all things Ian — the lore, the legends, and maybe a few things we just made up along the way with wavering accuracy.

Enjoy your stay in The Ian Zone.
